## WFMT Classical Music Stream

Listen to Chicago's [WFMT](https://www.wfmt.com/) classical music public radio stream.

## Description

Enjoy the finest free stream of classical music the internet has to offer.

## Commands

* Play WFMT
* Play classical music
* What's on WFMT?
* What was on WFMT?
